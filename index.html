Mass Media: todayandyesterday.co.uk is a special live transmission, streaming from Gustav Metzger’s exhibition Facing Extinction at Herbert Read Gallery, Canterbury, on the occasion of the Extinction Marathon: Visions of the Future.

Information

Gustav Metzger’s first digital work Mass Media: todayandyesterday.co.uk has been co-commissioned by The Serpentine Galleries and The SPACE to coincide with the Extinction Marathon: Visions of the Future, which Metzger has both inspired and co-curated. Throughout the two day broadcast, students in Canterbury will be seen selecting from a stack of thousands of newspapers installed at the heart of the gallery space, cutting and collaging articles across the walls in response to the subject of EXTINCTION. The transmission highlights Metzger's work Mass Media: Today and Yesterday as an installation in a continual state of flux and growth.
